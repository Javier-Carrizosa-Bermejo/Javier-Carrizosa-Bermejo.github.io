About me

My name is Javier Carrizosa Bermejo. Since I was a child I have loved videogames to the point of wanting to make them. That's why I embarked on the university adventure with a degree in computer science. I continued my professional career by taking a master's degree in video game development at the Complutense University of Madrid. Thanks to the master's degree, I was able to do an internship at Artax Games, a videogame company where I am currently working as a gameplay programmer.



Projects

Superfighters of Survival

Superfighters of Survival is a game developed using Unreal Engine 5 by Vortex Vision, a studio created for the Videogame Development Master's degree of the Complutense University of Madrid. I had the honor to work as a programmer dedicated mostly to combat mechanics, tools, sound and bug fixing.

Alan

Alan is an original game created by me in Unity 2021 for the end-of-degree project. All the content, including the art, design and script programming is completely original.
